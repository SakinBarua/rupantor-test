The Philosophy

RUPANTOR (রূপান্তর) means "transformation" in Bengali. The game embodies the universal truth that everything changes, nothing remains static. From the smallest seed to the mightiest mountain, from morning dew to evening clouds — all of nature is in constant flux.

রূপান্তর মানে পরিবর্তন। এই গেমটি সার্বজনীন সত্যকে মূর্ত করে যে সবকিছু পরিবর্তিত হয়, কিছুই স্থির থাকে না।

FAQ

Is this a matching game?

No! This is a transformation game. You play cards whose CATEGORY matches the World State's transform paths, not by matching colors or numbers.

What age is this for?

Ages 10 and up. The game teaches logical thinking and strategic planning.

How long does a game take?

Typically 15-30 minutes depending on player experience.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Once loaded, the game works completely offline using LocalStorage.
